    ABOUT US

    We are Lior Kobi & Tal Ben-Shimon
    This is a spacecraft war game in the spirit of the Eurovision. The player who represents Israel's representative to Eurovision can practice or manually select countries he wants to compete against. On the game screen, the player has the option to mute or play the background music.
